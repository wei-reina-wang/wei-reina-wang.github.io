About Me

I completed my Bachelor’s degree in Quantitative Finance at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (Shenzhen) from 2018 to 2022, during which I was a visiting student at the University of Oxford from 2020 to 2021. I then finished my Master’s program in Mathematics in Finance at the Courant Institute of Mathematical Sciences, New York University, in December 2024. Currently, I am pursuing a PhD at New York University under the supervision of Professor Katepalli Sreenivasan, with my research focusing on Fluid Dynamics and Quantum Computing.
